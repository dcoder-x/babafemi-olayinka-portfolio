Instacart Basket

Objective

To conduct exploratory analysis for Instacart Grocery, a US-based online grocery store. To derive insights into customer sales patterns, segment customers based on behavioural and demographic profiles to analyse spends, and then make recommendations for targeted marketing campaigns.

Constriants

Data supplied by Instacart is only from 2017 and some data had to be fabricated for the purposes of the project. A randomised 70% subset had to be used due to memory issues.
